# PRODUCT.md

register: brand

## What this is

Personal portfolio for Prateek Konduru, an AI Solutions Leader (Manager, AI
Solution Acceleration & Forward Deployment at Ushur). The site is a hiring
surface: its job is to make a recruiter, hiring manager, or enterprise
stakeholder conclude within 90 seconds that Prateek can lead enterprise AI
deployments.

## Users

- Recruiters and hiring managers for AI leadership / forward-deployed engineering roles
- Enterprise stakeholders and prospective clients checking credibility
- Peers arriving from LinkedIn or GitHub

They scan, they don't read. Numbers and role progression carry the argument.

## Brand voice

Precise, deployed, load-bearing. The concept hook is the job itself:
"forward deployed" read as a field-operations record. Confident and clinical,
never sci-fi cosplay, never startup-hype ("elevate", "seamless" are banned).

## Tone and aesthetic

Engineered ops console: graphite monochrome, one signal-amber instrument
color, expanded-width grotesk display type, mono data labels, a generative
canvas flow field as the only imagery. Committed dark theme (locked; no
light mode by design).

## Anti-references

- The AI-default portfolio: purple-to-cyan gradients, gradient text, glassmorphism cards, Inter
- Terminal/hacker green-on-black cosplay
- Editorial serif magazine affectation
- Stat-card strips, numbered section eyebrows, decorative status dots

## Strategic principles

- Content and IA are stable: anchors #about, #experience, #skills, #education, #contact are load-bearing (nav, SEO)
- Résumé facts must match dist/Profile.pdf; update both together
- One contact intent, one label: "Get in touch"
- Everything must survive JS-off, print, and prefers-reduced-motion
